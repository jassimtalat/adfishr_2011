Ad Formats

Display Ads

Display ads are graphical advertisements featured on web pages. They can appear in standard shapes and sizes for example, banner ads, skyscraper ads, large boxes and other various forms. Display ads are visually eye-catching to quickly grab the visitor's attention as they browse the page. Different forms of display ads include floating ads, expanding ads, wallpaper ads, pop-up/pop-under ads and video ads.
