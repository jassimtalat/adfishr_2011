Targeting Method

Contextual Advertising

Contextual advertising displays ads which match with the keywords on the page the visitor is browsing. It is a form of targeted advertising which matches the ads with the content of the page e.g. if you are browsing a sports website then there will be sport advertisements on the page like sports gear.

Behavioural Advertising

Behavioural advertising allows advertisers to target audiences based on their online web-browsing behaviour e.g. the pages they have recently visited or the keywords they have searched for in search engines. This is very effective, increasing ROI, because companies can target consumers who are in their market for their products and services.

Geo Targeting

Geo Targeting serve ads based on the web user's geographical location. This is very effective for companies who want to target customers from a specific location.
